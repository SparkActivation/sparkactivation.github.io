Adobe Spark Activation Process

  1. You have received an mail from Adobe Creative Cloud to activate Adobe Spark Premium license. (message@adobe.com)
  2. Click on that email to activate your adobe Spark license
  3. The mail will show the following text "You now have the access to Spark with Premium Features for K-12"
  4. Click on the Blue 'Get Started' Button
  5. Just sign in by clicking on "Get an Adobe ID" blue button.
  6. Put your first & last name & user ID (your email ID)
  7. Then create a new password, password must have mix of uppercase, lowercase, special character & numeric (e.g. Welcome@123)
  8. Accept the terms & conditions & click on "continue to India"
  9. Click on 9 dots (app launcher button) at the top right corner of the screen & click on Sp (spark logo)

    10. You are now landed to Spark page
